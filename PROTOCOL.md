You are forbidden from using:
- Fake assembly (opacity, fades, tricks)

### LAW 2 — EVERYTHING EXISTS IN SPACE
- All objects must be real 3D geometry
- All interactions must happen in world space
- The camera must physically move

You are forbidden from:
- overlays pretending to be objects
- UI-style transitions

### LAW 3 — CONTINUITY IS MANDATORY
- No cuts
- No resets
- No jumps

Everything must feel like:
- **one continuous journey**

### LAW 4 — PRECISION OVER SPEED

You must prioritize:
- correctness
- alignment
- realism

You are forbidden from:
- "good enough"
- approximations

### LAW 5 — FINAL QUALITY STANDARD

The result must feel like:

- immersive experience

If it feels like:
- a demo

- a placeholder

→ **it fails**

## SECTION 3 — SYSTEMS (IN ORDER OF CREATION)

You must build in this exact order.

### SYSTEM 1 — HEART FOUNDATION
**Requirements:**
- Use original heart model as base
- Maintain anatomical accuracy
- Maintain scale consistency

**FORBIDDEN:**
- Replacing with primitives
- Simplifying structure

### SYSTEM 2 — HEART SEGMENTATION (PUZZLE SYSTEM)
You must:
- Split the heart into real segments
- Each segment must:
  - match original mesh
  - fit perfectly with others
  - share exact boundaries

**PUZZLE RULE:**

When assembled:
- It must be identical to original heart
- No seams
- No gaps
- No mismatch

### SYSTEM 3 — ASSEMBLY ANIMATION
**Start State:**
- Segments scattered in space
- Positioned outside camera view

**Behavior:**

Each segment:
- Moves along curved path
- Rotates into alignment
- Locks precisely into place

**FORBIDDEN:**
- Fading pieces in/out
- Scaling tricks
- Teleporting

### SYSTEM 4 — SPATIAL SCROLL SYSTEM
You must:
- Map scroll → camera movement
- Move camera downward through space
- User must feel: **"I am traveling through a real environment"**

### SYSTEM 5 — ENVIRONMENT TRANSFORMATION
You must:
- Transition environments smoothly
- Morph between sections

**FORBIDDEN:**
- hard cuts
- disconnected scenes

### SYSTEM 6 — SPIRAL SYSTEM (CORRECTED)
You must:
- Space elements widely
- Control motion
- Maintain clarity

**Behavior:**
- Radius expands over time
- Motion is smooth
- No overlap clutter

### SYSTEM 7 — BODY EXIT SYSTEM
You must:
- Build layers:
  - inner anatomy
  - ribcage (3D geometry)
  - outer body
- Camera must:
  - Pass THROUGH layers
  - Not simulate exit

### SYSTEM 8 — HUMAN REVEAL
**Requirements:**
- Full human model
- Correct proportions
- Heart visible in chest

**Animation:**
- Face camera
- Thumbs-up
- Natural smile

**FORBIDDEN:**
- Primitive-looking humans
- Unnatural motion

### SYSTEM 9 — FINAL CINEMATIC
**Sequence:**
1. Heart fully assembled
2. Camera pulls back
3. Ribcage appears (3D)
4. Lungs + body context
5. Full human revealed
6. Final pose + emotion
